About Me

Event work is an act of service. As a professional in this field, it is my responsibility to plan and execute events that uplift my community. I feel lucky to pursue a career that allows me to give special memories to the people and communities I'm grateful for.

Currently, I am enrolled in NYU's M.S. in Event Management program. It has been a great opportunity to learn and grow. I look forward to leaving the program, as someone more capable of managing venues and events with consideration and compassion.

My passion for events stems from my love of music and performance. I have a theater background and played violin for 9 years. I'm an avid concert-goer and will jump at any opportunity to listen to live music. I particularly enjoy Kpop and Afrobeats.

At 24, I am hardworking, service-oriented and ambitious. My ultimate career goal is manage my own venue. I'm focused and ready to take the event world by storm.
